Capture Moments Studio

Site Plan Document

1. Site Name

Capture Moments Studio

Reason for the name: "Capture Moments Studio" was chosen because it perfectly encapsulates the essence of photography - preserving precious moments in time. The name is memorable, professional, and immediately communicates the studio's purpose. "Capture" suggests both the technical skill and the emotional connection of photography, while "Moments" emphasizes the timeless nature of the memories being preserved. The word "Studio" conveys professionalism and a dedicated space for creative work.

2. Site Purpose

The Capture Moments Studio website serves multiple purposes for both the business and its clients:

  • Portfolio Showcase: Display the photographer's best work across different categories (weddings, portraits, events, commercial) to attract potential clients and demonstrate artistic style and technical expertise.
  • Service Information: Provide detailed information about photography services, packages, pricing, and what clients can expect from their photo sessions.
  • Booking & Scheduling: Allow clients to easily book appointments, view availability, and manage their photography sessions online.
  • Client Gallery Access: Provide secure, password-protected galleries where clients can view, download, and share their photos after sessions.
  • About & Credentials: Share the photographer's story, experience, equipment, and professional background to build trust and credibility.
  • Contact & Location: Provide multiple ways for clients to get in touch and find the studio location for in-person consultations.

3. Scenarios

The following scenarios reflect the target audience users and their needs:

  1. Scenario 1: Engaged Couple Planning Wedding Photography
    Question: "We're getting married in 6 months and need to find a wedding photographer. We want to see examples of their work, understand their style, and know what packages they offer. Can we see their portfolio and get pricing information? We also want to know if they're available on our wedding date."
  2. Scenario 2: Family Looking for Professional Portraits
    Question: "Our family of 5 needs updated professional portraits for our holiday cards and family album. We want to see examples of family photography, understand the session process, and book an appointment. What should we wear and how long will the session take?"
  3. Scenario 3: Business Owner Needing Corporate Photography
    Question: "I need professional headshots for my team and some corporate event photography for our company website. Can I see examples of their commercial work and get a quote for multiple employees? I also need to know if they can come to our office location."
  4. Scenario 4: Previous Client Accessing Photos
    Question: "I had my wedding photos taken 2 weeks ago and want to see the proofs. How do I access my online gallery and when will my final edited photos be ready? I also want to order some prints and albums."
  5. Scenario 5: Event Organizer Seeking Event Photography
    Question: "I'm organizing a corporate conference and need professional event photography. Can I see examples of their event coverage and get pricing for a full-day event? I need both candid shots and formal group photos."

4. Color Schema

The following colors have been selected to create an elegant, professional, and timeless appearance suitable for a photography studio:

Primary Color

#1a1a1a (Deep Black)

Usage: Headers, navigation, footer background, primary text elements

Secondary Color

#f8f8f8 (Off-White)

Usage: Background sections, card backgrounds, subtle highlights

Accent Color

#d4af37 (Gold)

Usage: Call-to-action buttons, important highlights, luxury elements

Text Dark

#2c2c2c (Dark Gray)

Usage: Main body text, headings, primary content

Text Light

#666666 (Light Gray)

Usage: Secondary text, captions, metadata

Accent Light

#f4e4a6 (Light Gold)

Usage: Subtle highlights, hover states, background accents

Color Rationale:

  • Primary (Deep Black): Represents sophistication, elegance, and the timeless nature of photography. Black is also the traditional color of professional photography equipment.
  • Secondary (Off-White): Provides a clean, neutral background that doesn't compete with the photography content and creates a gallery-like atmosphere.
  • Accent (Gold): Adds luxury and premium feel, suggesting high-quality, professional photography services. Gold is associated with awards and excellence.
  • Text Colors: Dark gray for excellent readability while being softer than pure black, and light gray for secondary information.
  • Light Gold: Provides subtle warmth and luxury without being overwhelming.

5. Color Schema Use in Document

This site plan document demonstrates the color schema in action:

  • Primary Color (#1a1a1a): Used for the header background, main headings (h2), and wireframe headers
  • Secondary Color (#f8f8f8): Used for the main background and wireframe content areas
  • Accent Color (#d4af37): Used for heading underlines, wireframe borders, scenario list borders, and the header gradient
  • Text Dark (#2c2c2c): Used for main text content and headings
  • Text Light (#666666): Used for secondary text and descriptions
  • Accent Light (#f4e4a6): Used in the header gradient and subtle highlights

6. Typography Named and Used

Selected Fonts: Playfair Display & Open Sans

Reason for selection: Playfair Display is an elegant serif font perfect for headings and display text, conveying sophistication and artistic quality. Open Sans is a clean, highly readable sans-serif font ideal for body text and user interface elements. This combination creates a perfect balance between artistic elegance and professional readability.

Typography Examples:

Playfair Display Black (900): "Capture Moments Studio"
Playfair Display Bold (700): "Wedding Photography"
Playfair Display Regular (400): "Portrait Sessions"
Open Sans SemiBold (600): "Book Your Session"
Open Sans Regular (400): "Professional photography services for all your special moments"
Open Sans Light (300): "View our latest portfolio"

Typography Application:

  • Main Headings (h1, h2): Playfair Display Black (900) for strong visual impact
  • Section Headings (h3): Playfair Display Bold (700) for elegant hierarchy
  • Body Text: Open Sans Regular (400) for optimal readability
  • Navigation & Buttons: Open Sans SemiBold (600) for clear interaction elements
  • Subtle Text: Open Sans Light (300) for secondary information
